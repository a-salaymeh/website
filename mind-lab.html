MIND Lab

Machine Intelligence and Data Laboratory

The MIND Lab builds intelligent systems using machine learning, reinforcement learning, and data analytics, and applies them to autonomous systems, smart communities, and data-driven decision-making.

Mission. The lab develops machine learning and data methods for intelligent systems that people can understand and that hold up at scale. We work across fields and train students through real research projects.

Vision. Over the long term, the lab works toward systems in transportation, infrastructure, and communities that are more capable and fairer to the people who depend on them. How a system affects those people matters as much to us as how well it performs.

Machine Learning Reinforcement Learning Data Analytics Autonomous Systems Smart Communities

Research Themes

Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning

Cooperative and competitive multi-agent systems that learn optimal strategies through interaction.

Intelligent Transportation

AI for traffic signal control, congestion detection, and vehicle coordination to improve flow and safety.

Deep Learning

Neural architectures for perception, prediction, and generation across vision, time-series, and graph data.

Data Science & Analytics

Statistical modeling and data mining applied to large, heterogeneous datasets with a focus on interpretability.
