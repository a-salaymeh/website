MIND Lab

The MIND Lab, led by Dr. Areej Salaymeh, conducts research at the intersection of machine learning, data science, and intelligent systems — developing data-driven and AI methods that address real-world problems in transportation, business analytics, and decision-making.

Machine Learning Data Science Intelligent Systems Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning Intelligent Transportation

Research Themes

Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning

Cooperative and competitive multi-agent systems that learn optimal strategies through interaction.

Intelligent Transportation

AI for traffic signal control, congestion detection, and vehicle coordination to improve flow and safety.

Deep Learning

Neural architectures for perception, prediction, and generation across vision, time-series, and graph data.

Data Science & Analytics

Statistical modeling and data mining applied to large, heterogeneous datasets with a focus on interpretability.
