Welcome

I am an Assistant Professor of Information Technology and a researcher in machine learning, data science, and intelligent systems. My work explores how AI and data-driven methods can be applied to real-world problems in transportation, business analytics, and decision-making.

I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Wayne State University, where I previously spent over eight years as a lecturer and graduate academic advisor, supporting students across the Computer Science and Data Science & Business Analytics programs in setting their academic and career goals.

I am actively looking for motivated students and collaborators. If you are interested in working with me on machine learning, data science, or intelligent systems, please email me a copy of your CV.
