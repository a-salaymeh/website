Research

My work focuses on intelligent systems, data-driven decision-making, and applied machine learning.

Overview

Research Statement

My research sits at the intersection of machine learning, data science, and intelligent systems, with a focus on developing novel methods that are both theoretically grounded and practically impactful. I am particularly interested in how autonomous agents can learn to make effective decisions in complex, dynamic environments — and how these capabilities can be applied to domains such as transportation, business analytics, and social good.

A central thread of my work involves multi-agent reinforcement learning (MARL), where I study how networks of AI agents can coordinate and compete to discover optimal strategies. I apply these frameworks to intelligent transportation systems, exploring how vehicles and infrastructure can cooperate to reduce congestion, improve safety, and enhance energy efficiency.

Alongside this, I pursue applied work in deep learning and data science, collaborating with industry and academic partners to transform raw data into actionable insights across healthcare, finance, and supply chain domains.

Focus Areas

Research Areas

Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning

Designing cooperative and competitive multi-agent systems that learn through interaction. Focus on scalable algorithms, emergent communication, and safe exploration in shared environments.

MARL Game Theory Policy Optimization

Intelligent Transportation Systems

Applying AI to traffic signal control, autonomous vehicle coordination, and route optimization. Reducing congestion and improving safety through data-driven and RL-based approaches.

Autonomous Vehicles Traffic Flow ITS

Deep Learning

Building and training deep neural network architectures for perception, prediction, and generation tasks. Applications span image classification, time-series forecasting, and natural language processing.

Neural Networks CNN Transformers

Data Science & Analytics

Statistical modeling, feature engineering, and visualization techniques applied to large, heterogeneous datasets. Emphasis on interpretability and business-ready insights.

Predictive Modeling Data Mining Visualization

Business Analytics

Leveraging ML and statistical methods to support organizational decision-making, demand forecasting, customer segmentation, and risk analysis in business contexts.

Decision Support Forecasting BI

Data Mining

Discovering patterns, clusters, and associations in structured and unstructured data. Developing scalable algorithms for knowledge extraction from large-scale repositories.

Clustering Association Rules Pattern Recognition
